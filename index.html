About Us

WACM is Boston University student chapter of ACM-W, ACM's Women in Computing. We provide social, educational and outreach activities for women in the Computer Sciences department, in the university, and in the surrounding community.

Networking Events

We collaborate with companies to hold tech talks and networking events VIRTUALLY due to COVID-19.

Mentoring program

This program matches graduate mentors with undergraduate mentees to form one-on-one associations based on their areas of interest in CS. Mentors and mentees talk about course selection, grad school vs. industry, scholarship applications, interview preparations and volunteer opportunities and help them build a network of peers in computer science.
